What is the Green Star* Program?

The Green Star Program is a suite of tools designed to inspire and motivate awareness, action and behaviour change around the climate issue. Create teams to compete to reduce carbon emissions, waste or energy, and thus have a real impact on the environment.

Learn More

How does it work?

We inspire people to reduce their climate footprint by joining team competitions using our mobile friendly webapp. Based on world class behavioral psychology, these challenges encourage participants to reduce their carbon emissions by making small everyday changes to their routines. So, for example, they can eat less meat, drive less, lower the heating etc.


Small individual actions may not sound like much, but when lots of people do them, magic happens!

Because they are competing with other people in their organization, the gamified process is fun and engaging. At the end, the participants can compare their results, and the organizers receive a report outlining the carbon and financial savings that have been made.
